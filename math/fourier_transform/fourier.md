## Fourier Transforms

- Time to frequency

	$F(v) = \displaystyle\int \limits^{\infty}_{-\infty} f(t) e^{-2\pi i\nu t}dt$

- Frequency to time

	$f(t) = \displaystyle\int \limits_{-\infty}^{\infty} F(\nu) e^{2\pi i \nu t} d\nu$
